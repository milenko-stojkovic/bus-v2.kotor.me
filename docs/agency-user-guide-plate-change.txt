Agency user guide — Promjena tablica / Plate change (2026-06-25)
================================================================

Za puni opis funkcija v. docs/agency-panel.md i docs/agency-user-guide.md.

CG (dnevna naknada):
  Promjena tablice za dnevnu naknadu moguća je samo za buduće datume.
  Za tekući dan: Promjena tablice za dnevnu naknadu nije dostupna za tekući dan.

EN (Daily fee):
  Plate change for Daily fee is only available for future dates.
  On the current day: Plate change for Daily fee is not available on the current day.

Termini / time-slot reservations: unchanged rules (category, slot conflict).
